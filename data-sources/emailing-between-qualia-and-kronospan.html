The Qualia & Kronospan-CY Journey (Detailed)

A specific, evidence-based summary of our engagement, incorporating key requirements from the client's form submissions to build a truly targeted demo.

June 23, 2025

Initial Inquiry

Christoforos Georgiou from Kronospan-CY reaches out with a clear need for an on-premise AI solution to interact with their structured and unstructured data.

  • Core Need: AI agent/bot for internal users (15-30).
  • Key Constraint: Must be a fully local, secure system without cloud reliance.
  • Stated Tech Stack: Java, SWT, Oracle SQL, Apache Tomcat.
June 26, 2025

Initial Requirements Form

The client completed our secure form, providing the first layer of specific details for the project.

Technical & Project Specs:

  • Environment: Oracle 19c, Java 8 (Legacy), Tomcat 9.0 on a VM.
  • Deployment: Air-gapped/Isolated Network.
  • Scale: 15-20 users, 200-500 daily queries.
  • Timeline & Budget: 1-3 months, Budget > €15,000.

Specific Demo Request:

A key request was for flexible reporting from natural language:

"...structure the report with 1 column holding the descriptions, then populate the results in 5 extra columns per page until all requested info is included..."
Initial Stance: At this stage, the client explicitly requested we use generic, non-company data for the demo, stating: "No, please use generic examples".
July 3, 2025

The Enhanced Demo Proposal

Following a successful initial presentation, we proposed a paid, optional path to build a more relevant and valuable proof-of-concept.

  • Standard Demo: Shows core capabilities with generic data.
  • Enhanced Demo (€450): A custom-built, high-fidelity environment using realistic data structures, with the cost credited to the final project.
Strategic Move: Offering a paid, creditable demo filters for serious clients and justifies the resource investment for a truly tailored evaluation experience.
July 5 - 7, 2025

Agreement & The Data Pivot

The client agrees to the Enhanced Demo. Crucially, they now see the value in using their own data structures to make the demo as realistic as possible, prompting the need for an NDA.

The Turning Point: The client shifts from "use generic data" to "let's use our data structures." This is a massive increase in trust and a clear signal that they want to see a solution that solves their *exact* problems.
July 10, 2025

NDA Signed: Trust Formalized

We execute a mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement to legally protect the sensitive business logic and data structures they are about to share.

  • Disclosing Party: Banasino Investments Limited (Kronospan-CY).
  • Receiving Party: Fawzi Goussous (Qualia Solutions).
  • Purpose: To evaluate and build the "Kronospan AI BI Platform" demo.
Post-NDA

Kick-off: The Real Requirements

With legal protections in place, the client shares a highly detailed specification via a second form submission, forming the definitive blueprint for our demo.

Core Business Use Cases:

  • Loan Balances & Exposure: Track repayments, drawdowns, interest, etc., filterable by company, group, lender, and facility type (LTL, WCR, SWAPS).
  • Cash Liquidity: View working capital facilities, daily balance variances (>100k), and available undrawn funds by company/country.
  • Directorships: Report on directorships for selected people as of a specific date, filterable by country/group.

Sample User Queries:

  • Give me the loan Balance and Outstanding amount of all leases of Group A as at dd/mm/yyyy
  • All directorships of Person X as at dd/mm/yyyy
  • Working capital Facilities by Country as well as Cash balances and Available Undrawn funds

Target Hardware & Auth:

  • Servers: 2x GDX Spark on Ubuntu
  • CPU: 20 core Arm (10x Cortex-X925 + 10x Cortex-A725)
  • Memory/Storage: 128GB LPDDR5x RAM, 4TB NVMe
  • Auth: Custom user roles (Admin, Manager, User) for data access control.
Ready to Build: The combination of a paid commitment, an NDA, and these hyper-specific requirements gives us everything needed to build a demo that will resonate powerfully with their C-level executives.
